About Us

Homage

Till date we have provided homage to more then 5000 pets which include 3000 dogs and 2000 other pet animals. We aim to provide homage to 10 thousand pets by the end of 2022.

Care

We provide intensive care to the pets anf take care of each and needs for the healthy living of the pet and try to find peoples to adopt the pet as soon as possible.

Training

From last 2 years we started with the training programs for the pets as thier were cases where the customer and pet were finding difficulties to get familarised with each other.

Social Awareness

Every year we run number of campaigns against the buying of animals instead of adopting them and we have continuosly raised this issues on many plateform.

Customers

Till date we have more than 5000+ happy customers and we are glad to say that we are one of the leading organization who are promoting pet adoption culture.

Support

Our responsiblity is just not getting family for our pets but we also provide them life time supports through out the journey of our pet. To make sure the pets are well taken care.
